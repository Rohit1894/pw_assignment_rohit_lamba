## How to Use This Brief

1. Fill in every section above as specifically as possible.
2. Collect 3-5 sample answers you've written by hand (photos or typed reproductions).
3. Feed this brief + your samples to the AI with a prompt like:
   > "Here is my writing style profile and sample answers. Write a solution to [question] in my voice, matching my vocabulary, structure, mistakes, and formatting habits."
4. Compare the AI output to your real writing. Note what feels off and add those observations to Section 6.
5. Iterate — the brief improves each time you identify a gap.
